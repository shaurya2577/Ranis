Your Measurement Guide

Hi {{customer_name}},

Here's your step-by-step guide for taking accurate measurements for your {{garment_type}}. Take your time—precision matters.

Measurement Tips

  • Wear fitted clothing (not bulky layers)
  • Have someone help you measure
  • Use a soft measuring tape
  • Measure over your usual undergarments
  • Take each measurement twice to confirm

Required Measurements

Chest: Measure around the fullest part, keeping tape parallel to floor

Waist: Measure at natural waist (typically narrowest point)

Hip: Measure around fullest part, typically 7-9" below waist

Sleeve: From shoulder to wrist with arm slightly bent

Length: From shoulder to desired hem length
